# Scholarship Document Helper

This is a simple web app to help students prepare and format their documents and images for scholarship applications, especially for the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) in India.

## What it does
- Lets you crop, resize, and format images (like photos and signatures) to the exact requirements needed for scholarship forms.
- Uses face detection to automatically crop photos for passport-style images.
- Keeps all processing on your device—no uploads, so your data stays private.
- Provides quick links and info for the official NSP portal.

## Why I built it
I made this tool because a lot of students struggle with document formatting when applying for scholarships. This app makes it easy to get your files ready without needing Photoshop or any paid software.

## How to use
1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Choose the tool you need (crop, resize, etc.).
3. Follow the instructions to process your image or document.
4. Download the result and upload it to the scholarship portal.

## Note
This site is not affiliated with the official NSP portal. It’s just a helper tool to make document preparation easier.
